Short Biography

Dongjie Wang is currently reading Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Central Florida. He received his M.S. degree in Software Engineering from Southwest Jiaotong University in 2019, the B.S. degree Computer Science from Sichuan University in 2016.

His research interests include machine learning, deep learning and data mining, especially in spatio-temporal data mining, with applications to automated urban planning, human mobility modeling, outlier detection and network representation learning.

He interned in DiDi and JD.com for utilizing Artificial Intelligence to study consumer behavior. In addition, he worked in Joyy as an algorithm engineer for doing recommendation systems.
